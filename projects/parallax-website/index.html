What are Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are a form of digital currency that are created and managed through the use of advanced encryption techniques known as cryptography. The are alternate digital currencies that use decentralized control as opposed to centralized digital currency and central banking systems.

How are they decentralized

The decentralized control of each cryptocurrency works through distributed ledger technology, typically a blockchain that is a distributed database managed by a network of computers that could be operated by individual users, companies, or governments.
